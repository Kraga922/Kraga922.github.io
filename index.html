Presented a comprehensive, interpretability-driven jailbreaking audit of eight SOTA open-source LLMs (Llama-3, GPT-oss, Qwen, Phi). Introduced an adaptive two-stage grid search algorithm to identify optimal activation-steering coefficients for unsafe behavioral concepts.

ICCPS Submission
From Actions to Understanding: Conformal Interpretability of Temporal Concepts in LLM Agents

Proposed a framework for interpreting the temporal evolution of concepts in LLM agents through a step-wise conformal lens. Demonstrated that temporal concepts are linearly separable and aligned with task success in environments like ScienceWorld and AlfWorld.
